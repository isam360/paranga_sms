About Paranga Secondary School

Established in 1965, Paranga Secondary School has a long-standing tradition of academic excellence and holistic education. We are committed to developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared for the challenges of higher education and the modern world.

Our mission is to provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, socially, and morally. We foster an environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and responsible citizenship.

With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated staff, and a supportive community, we create an atmosphere where every student can thrive and succeed.

Academics & Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet the needs of all students and prepare them for higher education and career success.

8-4-4 Curriculum System

We follow the Kenyan 8-4-4 education system with emphasis on both academic excellence and practical skills development. Our curriculum is designed to cater to diverse student abilities and interests.

Core Subjects

English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, History, Geography, CRE

Technical Subjects

Computer Studies, Business Studies, Agriculture, Home Science, Art & Design

Co-curricular

Sports, Clubs, Societies, Leadership Training, Community Service

Performance

Consistently above national average in KCSE examinations

Transport Directions

From Nairobi: Take the Nairobi-Nakuru highway, exit at Limuru junction, proceed 15km towards Paranga town. The school is 2km from the town center.

Public Transport: Matatus are available from Nairobi to Paranga town. From there, boda-boda or tuk-tuk services can bring you to the school gate.
